Bridging inequality with education

Mission

Appropriate Development Technology, Inc. (ADT) is a nonprofit based in Houston, Texas on a mission to close the digital divide. Through technical training, we empower people, of all walks of life, to better their lives. Inside our Internet Learning Centers and Maker Spaces, we introduce emerging technologies and learning resources for career advancement. We believe that knowledge and skill development will open professional opportunities and a path to financial security.
